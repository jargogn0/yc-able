How it works

1

Drop your data

Upload a CSV, Excel, or JSON file. Or paste a URL. The AI instantly profiles it — rows, columns, types, nulls, distributions, potential targets.

2

Describe your goal

Say "predict churn", "forecast revenue", or just "do your best". The AI infers the task, target, metric, and builds a research plan.

3

Watch it work

The AI writes training code, runs experiments, parses real metrics, decides KEEP or DISCARD, auto-fixes crashes, and iterates. All narrated live.

4

Deploy in one click

Get your model as a deploy-ready API server with FastAPI, Docker, and Railway config. Or download the model, explore feature importance, share results.
